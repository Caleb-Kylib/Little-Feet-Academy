From the Heart

A Message from our Director

Welcome to Little Feet Academy. When I founded this institution, my vision was simple: to create a space where every child feels seen, heard, and loved. We believe that early childhood is the most critical window for fostering a lifelong love for learning.

"Our mission is not just to teach, but to ignite a curiosity that lasts forever."

Every member of our team is hand-picked not just for their qualifications, but for their genuine passion for children. Together, we are building more than a school; we are building a foundation for the leaders of tomorrow.

Dr. Sarah Mwangi
Director & Founder, Little Feet Academy
