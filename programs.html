The CBC Advantage

At Little Feet Academy, we fully embrace the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). Our approach moves beyond rote memorization to focus on what a child can DO.

  • ✅ Critical Thinking: Encouraging kids to ask "Why?" and "How?"
  • ✅ Creativity: Nurturing innate talents through art, music, and play.
  • ✅ Communication: Building confidence in self-expression.
  • ✅ Collaboration: Working together to solve problems.

Our Philosophy

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." We believe in identifying and nurturing the unique potential of every child through a learner-centered approach.
