About Us

Nurturing young minds with care, creativity, and curiosity since 2010.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Little Feet Academy began as a cozy, family-run daycare with a big dream: to make early education joyful, caring, and creative for every child. Over the years, we’ve grown into a cherished learning community, partnering with families and nurturing hundreds of little learners. Our approach blends play-based exploration with essential early skills, helping children build confidence, curiosity, and kindness.
