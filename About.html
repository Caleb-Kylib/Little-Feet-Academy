About Our Journey

Nurturing young minds with care, creativity, and curiosity since 2010.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Little Feet Academy began as a cozy, family-run daycare with a big dream: to make early education joyful, caring, and creative for every child.

Over the years, we’ve grown into a cherished learning community, partnering with families and nurturing hundreds of little learners. Our approach blends play-based exploration with essential early skills, helping children build confidence, curiosity, and kindness.

The CBC Advantage

At Little Feet Academy, we fully embrace Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC).

Unlike traditional systems, CBC focuses on what a student can do rather than just what they know. Our curriculum is designed to identify and nurture the unique talents and interests of every child, ensuring they are not just exam-ready, but life-ready.
