Pre-Primary Education

PP1 & PP2 Program

Ages 4 to 6: joyful learning that prepares children for primary school success.

🎨

Building Foundations

In PP1 and PP2, children transition from play-centered learning to more structured classroom experiences. We build strong early skills in literacy, numeracy, communication, and personal responsibility.

Our teachers use songs, phonics, manipulatives, drawing, and guided activities to make learning clear and enjoyable. Learners gain confidence while developing attention span and classroom routines.

By the end of pre-primary, children are well prepared for Lower Primary with the academic readiness and social maturity needed for the next stage.

Program Highlights

  • Social & Life Skills
