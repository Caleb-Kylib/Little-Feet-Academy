Admission Process

A simple, transparent path to joining Little Feet Academy.

Fill Application Form

Visit the school to pick a form or download it here. Fill it in and submit at our front office.

Interview At School

We will schedule a physical interview for your child to understand their unique needs and placement.

Interview Feedback

Our team will review the interview results and provide detailed feedback to the parents within 48 hours.

Admissions Letter

Successful applicants will be invited to pick up their official admission letter and joining instructions.
